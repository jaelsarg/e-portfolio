International Project

For my international project, I went to Poland. The theme of the project was: "Low code business". There I worked together with the students from University of Economics in Katowice. In groups of 4 people (2 IT students and 2 business students) we had to create a low code / no code solution that would solve a problem.

We had to think of a business plan, but also create a website and a mobile app. It was a very nice project. We learned a lot by working together with peopel from other fields. Also the no code/low code part was very interesting, because it was something I never tried before.

But we did more than just working on the project while we were in Poland. On the first day we had an international buffet, where we could try food/drinks from Poland and Belgium. We also went bowling with the whole group, and on the last day we went to eat pizza.

The project was very nice, and I am glad I had the opportunity to do it. It was a great experience, and I learned a lot from it.
