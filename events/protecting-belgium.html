Protecting Belgium: Play a role in active cyber protection

📍 Howest Brugge, Belgium
📅 16/12/2025 - 19:00-20:30
🎤 Tom Hermant, Sandro Manzo, Lt Kol Timmie Bonneu

This one-time event, held from 19:00 to 20:30, focused on cybersecurity careers and the role of cybersecurity within national security. It was aimed at individuals interested in exploring job opportunities in the field and gaining a better understanding of how cybersecurity organizations operate.

The session featured three experienced speakers from different areas of the cybersecurity landscape. Tom Hermant, an IT recruiter at EgovSelect, provided insights into the job market and what employers look for in candidates. Sandro Manzo, Head of the Cyber Threat Research Intelligence Sharing (CyTRIS) department at the Centre for Cybersecurity Belgium, shared his expertise on threat intelligence and the role of national cybersecurity organizations. Lt. Col. Timmie Bonneu, working in cyber defense within the Belgian Defence, offered a perspective on cybersecurity in a military and national defense context.

The event covered key cybersecurity organizations, their missions, and how they contribute to national security. It also provided a clear overview of the different career paths available within cybersecurity, along with the skills and qualifications required to pursue them.

As someone without prior experience in cybersecurity, I found the session educational and informative. I learned a lot about the structure of the field and the variety of roles it offers. However, I also realized that this domain is not the area that interests me most compared to other fields within IT.

The event itself was well organized, and the speakers offered valuable, real-world insights. While I would not attend a similar session again due to my personal interests, I would still recommend it to others who are curious about cybersecurity or considering a career in this field.
