About Me

Hi, I’m Jaël Sargant, a student of Applied Computer Science with a focus on becoming a software engineer. I’m interested in both front-end and back-end development, and I enjoy working on full-stack projects where I can combine different parts of the application into a cohesive whole.

I see my key strengths as problem-solving and being eager to learn. I enjoy the process of learning new technologies and watching everything come together in the final result. Throughout my studies, I’ve worked on several projects, including team-based development work, and I’ve also attended four events — two hackathons and a few other sessions — which have helped me grow both technically and collaboratively.

Outside of programming, I enjoy creative hobbies such as puzzling and playing the piano. These activities help me stay curious, patient, and thoughtful — qualities that I also bring to my work as a developer.

I’m always looking for opportunities to learn, collaborate, and build meaningful digital solutions.
